About Us

Alsuro is a leading supplier of industrial automation solutions and equipment for various industries. We specialize in supplying new, refurbished, and discontinued components, ensuring seamless operation for enterprises. Our product range includes programmable logic controllers (PLCs), frequency converters, sensors, operator panels, control systems, and much more.

Our mission is to provide reliable and innovative automation solutions, helping businesses enhance efficiency, reduce costs, and minimize downtime risks. We collaborate with leading global manufacturers, guaranteeing high-quality products, competitive prices, and prompt delivery.

Engineering and Development

In addition to equipment supply, Alsuro offers engineering and implementation services for automated control systems. We develop comprehensive solutions, including:

  • Design and modernization of automation systems
  • Selection and integration of equipment
  • Development of software for process control
  • Implementation of intelligent monitoring and data analysis systems
  • Technical support and maintenance services

Our team of experts assists clients in adapting production processes to meet modern market demands, enhancing their reliability and efficiency.

Strong Presence in Africa

Alsuro actively operates in the African market, providing industrial automation solutions to companies across the continent. We understand the specific challenges and opportunities in the region and offer tailored solutions to help businesses optimize operations, improve productivity, and achieve sustainable growth. Our extensive experience working with African enterprises allows us to deliver high-quality products and services that meet local market needs.

We strive for long-term partnerships with our clients by providing customized solutions, expert consultations, and high-quality service.
